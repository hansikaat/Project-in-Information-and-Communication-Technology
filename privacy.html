Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 20, 2019

Hunter Talk ("us", "we", or "our") operates the Hunter Talk mobile application (the "Service").

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

We use your data to provide and improve the Service. By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

What data do we collect?

Hunter Talk collects the following data:

  • Email address
  • Username

Our service uses Google Firebase, which may collect data such as:

  • Password
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • User agent
  • IP address

The user can also optionally consent to sharing GPS location information in order to use our map service.

How do we collect your data?

You directly provide Hunter Talk with most of the data we collect. We collect data and process data when you register for our Service. Hunter Talk may also receive your data indirectly from the following sources:

  • GPS location, when enabled
  • Information collected by Google Firebase to maintain database functionality and authorize users

How will we use your data?

Hunter Talk collects your data so that we can:

  • Manage your account
  • Email you when new password is requested
  • Identify you to other users

If you agree, Hunter Talk will use your location data to share your location with other users in your Hunter Talk group.

How do we store your data?

Hunter Talk securely stores your data with the service Google Firebase. Hunter Talk will keep your data from the moment you register and for up to 180 days after your account is deleted by us or after you request that we delete it.

Security of Data

The security of your data is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Transfer of Data

Your information may be transferred to — and maintained on — computers located outside of your state, province, country or other governmental jurisdiction where the data protection laws may differ than those from your jurisdiction.

If you are located outside Sweden and choose to provide information to us, please note that we transfer the data, including Personal Data, to Sweden and process it there.

What are your data protection rights?

Hunter Talk would like to make sure you are fully aware of all of your data protection rights. Every user is entitled to the following:

The right to access - You have the right to request Hunter Talk for copies of your personal data.

The right to rectification - You have the right to request that Hunter Talk correct any information you believe is inaccurate. You also have the right to request Hunter Talk to complete the information you believe is incomplete.

The right to erasure - You have the right to request that Hunter Talk erase your personal data, under certain conditions.

The right to restrict processing - You have the right to request that Hunter Talk restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.

The right to object to processing - You have the right to object to Hunter Talk’s processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.

The right to data portability - You have the right to request that Hunter Talk transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.

If you make a request, we have one month to respond to you. If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at our email above.
